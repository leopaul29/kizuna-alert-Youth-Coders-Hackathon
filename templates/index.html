About Kizuna Alert

Kizuna (ÁµÜ) means "bond" or "connection" in Japanese. This application connects members of the same neighborhood to share resources and support each other during natural disasters.

Join Your Mutual Aid Group

Enter your Japanese postal code to join your neighborhood's mutual aid group and access the community chat.

Examples: 100-0001 (Chiyoda), 150-0001 (Shibuya), 160-0001 (Shinjuku)

How It Works

  • 1. Join - Enter your postal code to join your neighborhood group
  • 2. Communicate - Share resources and ask for help in the chat
  • 3. Check-in - Signal that you're safe to reassure your neighbors
  • 4. Support - Help your community during emergencies
